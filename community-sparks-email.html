Community Spark Scholarship.

During this holiday season, as you consider a worthy cause for your end of the year gift, we're asking you to consider the credit union industry's legacy and how you can invest in the future. We cannot let financial barriers keep our brightest and most talented youth from experiencing the transformative power of a degree.

In 2021, Alabama and Georgia credit unions joined forces with the Southeastern Credit Union Foundation (SECUF) to create the Community Spark Scholarship. The scholarship provides financial assistance to underserved/first-generation college students attending a four-year accredited college in Alabama or Georgia.

In 2022, we awarded two $40,000 scholarships to young gentlemen who demonstrated hard work, innovation, and leadership. The generous scholarship allows students from low economic backgrounds to attend college free from the worry of additional costs such as tuition, textbooks, housing, food, and transportation.

To expand the reach of the Community Spark Scholarship, we are establishing an endowment fund goal of $2 million to provide scholarships of $40,000 each to ten students by 2025. The fund will provide us with the resources to fuel the Community Spark Scholarship's mission for long-term success.

You can play a part. Credit unions have always been about "People Helping People." The Community Spark Scholarship is the perfect example of the credit union movement's belief in collaboration for the benefit of our members and communities and proves that we are stronger together.

We hope you will join our new endeavor, the Community Spark Scholarship Endowment Fund, to support the next generation of leaders. To give or for more information visit us at https://www.lscu.coop/foundation/community-sparks.php.
